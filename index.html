Analyzing Canadian&American Legislators and Policy Areas

● Objective: was to find how politicians cluster around policy topics, and how this clustering relates to a congress member’s ability to pass bills into law. ● Leveraged: K-means and Gaussian mixture model to cluster topics of interest and t-SNE algorithm to visualize data. Data is High-dimensional, complex and stored in a different JSON file. ● Result: found that the legislators’ interest in policy areas is an indicator of multiple factors such as legislative performance, political position and even geographical location
